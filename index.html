WHAT IS "PROJECT: PIONEER"?

Businesses typically rely on multiple software applications to coordinate an organization and produce, deliver, invoice and receive payment for a good or service (“Order to Cash").

When the applications are not digitally connected (“integrated"), the business data is segmented making it difficult to make accurate data-driven decisions.

The objective of this project is to venture ('pioneer') beyond the legacy business applications by implementing an Enterprise Resource Planning system ("ERP"), provided by Epicor.

As a result, Holt Manufacturing will have an integrated solution to help manage end-to-end processes and translate consolidated business data into useful, actionable information.
